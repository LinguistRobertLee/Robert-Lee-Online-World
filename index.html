About

Robert Donghyun Lee is currently a PhD student in King's College London specialised in international relations. With a decade of professional experience in UNESCO, he focuses on the subject on Northeast Asian regional dynamics concerning UNESCO's heritage designation.

Based on theoretical foundation and abundant field experience, he looks forward to contribute to making better world as a reliable and irreplaceable specialist guiding desirable direction to the public around the globe.
